The Adept's Druid Robe is a Tier 4 Cloth Armor which may be obtained by crafting or via the Market Place.
There are three options for the R spell slot: Mend Wounds (Fifth), Frost Shield (Fifth) and Obsessive Burst (Fifth)

There are four options for the passive slot: Aggression (Passive), Concentration (Passive), and Efficiency (Passive)  
Additionally, the Adept's Druid Robe has +40% to Physical and Magic Attack Bonus, as well as a +40% to Physical and Magical Ability Bonus

The Adept's Druid Robe may have different Item Quality ranging from Normal to Masterpiece

The Adept's Druid Robe Item Quality may be increased at a Repair Station for a fee
